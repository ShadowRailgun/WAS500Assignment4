World's Edge Library

Welcome to the library

Everyone is welcome. Book trading is encouraged. If you do not wish to interact with people we have a nook where books can be left to be picked up by a new soul.

The navigation pane to the left will help you reach the other pages for the website.

Library Rules

No music played without earbuds

Do not take books home without checking them out as that is theft (taking a book from the nook without leaving a book also counts)

If you have any liquids with you they must have a lid

If you ruin any property you will pay for it to be replaced

If you break any of the rules you will be permanently banned or chucked off Earth if you prefer! Don't forget to have a good day! :)
